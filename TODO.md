- **Target Users**: Homeowners, businesses, organizations seeking security solutions in Ghana and West Africa

## Page Structure & Requirements

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Logo (text-based: SETCON SECURITY)
- Links: Home, About Us, Services, Products, Portfolio, Sales, Contact
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Sticky navigation

### 2. Home Page Sections
- Hero section with background image overlay
- Company tagline
- WhatsApp link button (prominent)
- Quick contact info
- Services overview highlights

### 3. About Us Page
- Company history and background
- Mission and vision statements
- Why choose us / value propositions
- Team or expertise section

### 4. Services Page
- List all services with icons/descriptions:
  - CCTV Systems
  - Intruder Alarms
  - Fire Alarms
  - Electric Fencing
  - Slide Gate Automation
  - Swing Gate Automation
  - Garage Door Automation
  - Motion Door Sensor Automation
  - Tensiles
  - Solar Systems
  - Remote Monitoring Services
  - Cloud Backup Storage

### 5. Products Page
- Grid display of security products
- Product categories
- Product specifications/features

### 6. Portfolio Page
- Gallery of completed projects
- Project images/descriptions
- Client testimonials (optional)

### 7. Sales Page
- Special offers/promotions
- Package deals
- Contact for quotes CTA

### 8. Contact Page
- Contact information (phone, email, address)
- **Enquiry Form** (must be functional):
  - Name field
  - Email field
  - Phone field
  - Service interest dropdown
  - Message textarea
  - Submit button (formspree or similar service)
- Map integration (optional)
- Business hours

### 9. Footer (All Pages)
- Logo
- Quick links
- Social Media Links:
  - Facebook
  - Instagram
  - Twitter/X
  - LinkedIn
  - WhatsApp
- Copyright notice

## Technical Requirements
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first, works on all devices
- **User Friendly**: Easy navigation, clear CTAs
- **Form Functionality**: Using Formspree for email submission
- **Performance**: Fast loading, optimized images
- **Accessibility**: Alt tags, proper contrast, keyboard navigation
